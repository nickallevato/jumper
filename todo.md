# Jumper — Ideas Backlog

## Puzzles
- **Floor sequence** — a set of pressure-plate tiles that must be stepped on in a hidden order; wrong order resets, correct order opens a door or reveals a secret passage. No UI hint — players figure it out by watching the floor react.
- **Mirror relay** — bounce a light beam between reflective items placed by different players to activate a distant switch. Each player carries one mirror; positioning matters.

## Dungeons / Rooms
- **The Sunken Library** — mid-size dungeon, dark background, floating book-stack platforms at varying heights that drift slowly up and down. Unlocked by diving into a specific cracked floor tile in the overworld. Lantern item reveals hidden shelf platforms.
- **The Belltower** — a single tall vertical shaft, 4 tiles wide, with tiny ledges spiraling up the walls. Players wall-kick their way to the top. First to reach the bell triggers a world event visible to all players in the overworld.
